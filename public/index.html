Personalized Nutrition for NYC Individuals and Families

Rooted in Clinical Expertise and Cultural Understanding

As a Registered Dietitian and college nutrition professor in NYC, I specialize in translating complex science into practical, everyday meals. My practice provides Medical Nutrition Therapy (MNT) for conditions such as diabetes, hypertension, and PCOS, alongside a compassionate, non-restrictive approach to weight and chronic condition management.

I don't believe in erasing your cultural food traditions to improve your health. Instead, I use evidence-based strategies that honor your heritage while helping you reach meaningful, sustainable health goals.

Nutrition should support your life—not take it over.

Meet the Dietitian Behind Dietitian New York

Merve Ciray, MS, RDN, CDN

College Professor, Multicultural Mom, and Your Partner in Health

"As a college professor, I know the science. As a mom to a toddler and a woman of a multicultural background, I know the reality. I don't believe in 'one-size-fits-all' diets because your nutrition should be as unique as your DNA and as vibrant as your culture."
The Professor: I translate complex clinical research into bite-sized, actionable steps.
The Mom: I understand the "toddler-era" chaos and the need for quick, nourishing family meals.
The Global Citizen: My multicultural background with my Master education in Global kitchen; I celebrate diverse cuisines —no "food shaming" or cutting out cultural staples here.

How We Work Together

Choose the program that fits your needs. Each consultation is personalized to you, respecting your culture, lifestyle, and health goals.

Foundations Package (3 Months)

Most Popular

Build clarity, confidence, and sustainable habits. Ideal if you're ready to make meaningful changes but want structure and expert guidance without overwhelm.

  • ✓ Personalized nutrition assessment & goal setting during initial consultation ~60 mins
  • ✓ Individualized meal plan, updated bi-weekly depending on current needs
  • ✓ Bi-weekly follow-up sessions ~30 mins
  • ✓ Total of 6 virtual appointments
  • ✓ Evidence-based Medical Nutrition Therapy (MNT) as needed
  • ✓ Non-restrictive weight or condition-focused support
  • ✓ Practical meal strategies that respect your culture & lifestyle
  • ✓ Ongoing support, accountability, and adjustments
